General Information

What is PaySurity?

PaySurity is a comprehensive payment solutions provider offering transparent payment processing, point-of-sale systems, and merchant services for businesses of all sizes. We focus on providing affordable, secure payment technology with exceptional customer service.

How long does it take to get started with PaySurity?

Most merchants can be up and running with PaySurity in as little as 24-48 hours. Our streamlined application process and dedicated onboarding team ensure a smooth transition to our payment solutions. Complex businesses may require additional time for custom integrations or setup.

What types of businesses do you serve?

PaySurity serves businesses across a wide range of industries, including:

  • Restaurants and food service
  • Retail stores
  • E-commerce businesses
  • Service providers
  • Legal firms
  • Healthcare providers
  • Nonprofit organizations

Our solutions are customizable to meet the specific needs of your industry.

What types of payments can I accept with PaySurity?

PaySurity enables you to accept:

  • All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Mobile wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay)
  • ACH payments
  • Contactless payments
  • Cryptocurrency
  • Buy Now, Pay Later options

Our goal is to let your customers pay however they prefer.

PaySurity stands out from other payment processors in several key ways:

  • Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees or complex pricing structures.
  • No Long-Term Contracts: We don't lock you into agreements with early termination fees.
  • Next-Day Funding: Get your money faster than with most providers.

Pricing & Billing

How does your pricing work?

We offer transparent, flexible pricing based on your business type and processing volume. Unlike many processors, we don't hide fees in fine print or complex statements. You'll know exactly what you're paying for, with rates starting as low as 0.15% + $0.07 per transaction. Contact our sales team for a customized quote tailored to your business.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. We believe in earning your business every day, which is why we don't lock you into long-term contracts or charge early termination fees. You're free to cancel at any time without penalty.

Are there any setup fees?

No, PaySurity does not charge setup fees for standard implementations. For businesses requiring complex custom integrations, there may be professional services fees, which will be clearly disclosed before any work begins.

How quickly will I receive my funds?

We offer next-day funding as standard for all merchants. Funds from transactions processed before the daily cutoff time (typically 11:59 PM EST) will be deposited into your account the next business day. Same-day funding options are also available for eligible merchants.

How often will I be billed for your services?

Processing fees are deducted at the time of transaction. Any monthly subscription fees for POS software or additional services are billed monthly on the anniversary of your account activation. You'll receive detailed monthly statements showing all transactions and fees.

POS Systems

What POS systems does PaySurity offer?

PaySurity offers three primary POS solutions:

  • Bistro Beast: Designed for restaurants, cafes, and food service businesses
  • GrocerEase: Tailored for grocery stores and supermarkets
  • Ecom-ready Retail: Built for retail businesses with both physical and online presence

Each system can be customized with additional features and modules to meet your specific business needs.

Do I need to purchase hardware to use your POS systems?

While our software can run on many existing devices, we offer a complete range of POS hardware designed to work seamlessly with our systems. This includes terminals, tablets, card readers, receipt printers, cash drawers, and barcode scanners. Our team can help you determine what hardware best fits your needs, and we offer both purchase and lease options.

Can your POS systems work offline?

Yes, all of our POS systems include offline mode functionality. This allows you to continue processing sales even if your internet connection is temporarily unavailable. Once connectivity is restored, the system will automatically sync all transaction data with our servers.

Do your POS systems integrate with other business software?

Absolutely. Our POS systems integrate with a wide range of business software, including:

  • Accounting systems (QuickBooks, Xero, Sage)
  • Inventory management platforms
  • Customer relationship management (CRM) tools
  • E-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento)
  • Employee scheduling and payroll systems
  • Loyalty program platforms

We also offer an API for custom integrations with your existing systems.

How do I get training on your POS systems?

We provide comprehensive training options to ensure you and your staff can make the most of your POS system:

  • Initial onboarding training (included with all plans)
  • On-site training sessions (available for an additional fee)
  • Remote training webinars
  • Self-service video tutorials and documentation
  • Regular feature update training

Our goal is to make sure you feel comfortable and confident using your system from day one.

What should I do if my card terminal isn't working?

If you're experiencing issues with your card terminal, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Restart the terminal by powering it off and on
  2. Check all cable connections
  3. Verify your internet connection is working
  4. Run a test transaction in training mode

If issues persist, contact our technical support team for immediate assistance.
