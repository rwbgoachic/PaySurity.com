Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

How long does it take to get started with PaySurity?

Most merchants can be up and running with PaySurity in as little as 24-48 hours. Our streamlined application process and dedicated onboarding team ensure a smooth transition to our payment solutions.

What types of payments can I accept with PaySurity?

PaySurity enables you to accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), mobile wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), ACH payments, and even cryptocurrency. Our goal is to let your customers pay however they prefer.

How does your pricing work?

We offer transparent, flexible pricing based on your business type and processing volume. Unlike many processors, we don't hide fees in fine print or complex statements. You'll know exactly what you're paying for, with rates starting as low as 0.15% + $0.07 per transaction. Contact our sales team for a customized quote.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. We believe in earning your business every day, which is why we don't lock you into long-term contracts or charge early termination fees. You're free to cancel at any time without penalty.

How quickly will I receive my funds?

We offer next-day funding as standard for all merchants. Funds from transactions processed before the daily cutoff time (typically 11:59 PM EST) will be deposited into your account the next business day. Same-day funding options are also available for eligible merchants.
